Who We Are

A Team That Treats Your
Business Like Our Own

OS World Solutions was founded with a single belief: businesses shouldn't have to choose between quality and cost. We built a team of specialists in insurance operations, accounting, digital marketing, technology and AI automation — people who genuinely understand the industries they serve.

Based in Udaipur, Rajasthan, we serve clients primarily in the US and UK — handling the operational workload that keeps businesses running, so our clients can focus entirely on growth, relationships, and strategy.

Today we're a 25+ person team across multiple disciplines, operating with the precision of a large firm but the responsiveness of a dedicated in-house team.
